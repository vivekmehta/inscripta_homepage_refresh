What is ParseQa Curate?

ParseQa Curate is a state-of-the-art system for contextual data extraction & curation from a wide variety of documents. Extract data into Excel/CSV/JSON, or simply ask questions to get relevant answers from mountains of large documents in a matter of minutes.
