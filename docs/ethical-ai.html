Ethical AI: Why?

To say that AI affects society and that unethical software could be responsible for worsening injustice, is stating the obvious. The sheer scale that can be achieved by AI solutions impacts thousands of people at lightning speed. Built using datasets that have embedded biases, Machine Learning systems often have unintended effects on already disadvantaged groups.
Unfortunately, there is no magic bullet or algorithm to rid your AI of biases. You clean your datasets, measure the bias in the decisions and hire conscientious data scientists.
